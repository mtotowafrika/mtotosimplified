Our Aim

The aim of Mtoto W’afrika is to bring hope and love to vulnerable children and to transform poor communities in Uganda.

Our Vision

A brighter future for every African child, raised to be resilient, resourceful and a Godly community leader.

Our Mission

Restoring hope, Inspiring talents, & Grooming an exceptional, intentional & generational African child, while offering support through bringing resources closer to community reach.

MCCM Bible Verse

“Train up a Child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it” Proverbs 22:6.
