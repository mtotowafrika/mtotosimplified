Empowering Farmers: Training & Practical Application

Our hands-on training programs equip community members with the knowledge and tools to implement Farming God's Way effectively.

Mtoto W'afrika proudly facilitated comprehensive training in Farming God's Way, providing essential tools and knowledge. This initiative empowers members to cultivate their land effectively. While initial plans included a communal garden, challenges with open property and animal trespass have led to a focus on individual farming, ensuring greater success and food security for families.

Beyond agriculture, Mtoto W'afrika's steadfast support—spanning spiritual guidance, financial aid, material resources, and skill development—has ignited a wave of entrepreneurial spirit. Our members are now proficient in a variety of income-generating activities:

  • Table Cloth Making
  • Toasting Fresh Fish for Muchomo
  • Making Mandazi (Sweet Buns)
  • Sewing Clothes (by Malaika Girls)
  • Crafting Reusable Sanitary Pads
  • Expert Sweater Weaving
