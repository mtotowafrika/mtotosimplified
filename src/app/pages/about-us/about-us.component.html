Our Aim

To bring hope and love to vulnerable children and transform poor communities in Uganda.

Our Vision

"Raise Resilient, Resourceful, and Community-based Godly Leaders for Africa’s Tomorrow Today."

Mission Statement

Restoring Hope, Inspiring Talents, And Grooming an Exceptional African Child, While Offering Support Through Bringing Resources Closer to Community Research.

MCCM Bible Verse

“Train up a Child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it” Proverbs 22:6.

Guiding Principles

Community-led and driven, accountability and transparency, clear communication, and support appreciation, with a strong focus on child protection.
