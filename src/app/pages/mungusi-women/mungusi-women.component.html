Transforming Lives in Maiaciku Village

At Mtoto W'afrika, we believe in the power of women and girls to drive change within their communities. Our initiatives in Maiaciku village are designed to foster economic independence, provide essential education, and nurture spiritual growth, creating a ripple effect of positive transformation.

The Mungusi Women's Savings Group

A cornerstone of empowerment, fostering financial literacy and community support.

Established in 2023, this group provides a vital platform for 25 women, 15 Malaika girls, and 3 boys, totaling 43 members, to collectively save, access credit, and receive crucial financial literacy training. Through their dedication, the group has collectively saved an impressive average of 9,000,000 UGX annually. This remarkable achievement is built on weekly savings and individual interest generation, demonstrating their commitment to self-sufficiency.

Despite challenges arising from the diverse composition of the group, our unwavering commitment is to uplift every single member, mirroring the transformative support Mtoto W'afrika provides to the entire community.

  • Financial Independence
  • Strong Community Bonds
  • Personal & Spiritual Growth

The Malaika Girls Group: Nurturing Future Leaders

Empowering young girls with mentorship, education, and vital life skills.

The Malaika Girls Group is a sanctuary where young girls receive invaluable mentorship, comprehensive life skills training, and dedicated educational support. This initiative is crucial in empowering them to remain in school, resist the pressures of early marriage, and blossom into confident, self-reliant young women who will shape the future of Maiaciku.

  • Dedicated Mentorship & Guidance
  • Comprehensive Education & Skill Development
  • Cultivating Empowerment & Self-Confidence
